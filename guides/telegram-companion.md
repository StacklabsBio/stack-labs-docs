# Telegram companion

The Stack Labs Telegram companion provides a lighter way to reach selected daily actions. It complements the full App rather than replacing it.

## Open the companion

Start from the official [Stack Labs Telegram bot](https://t.me/StackLabsBioBot). Follow the linking instructions shown by the App and Telegram. Do not share a linking code or authenticated link with another person.

## What to expect

After a successful link, Telegram can show the selected Today experience and supported check-in or reminder actions. The full App remains the place for broader stack, Labs, insight, and account workflows.

## Stop or unlink

Use the current Telegram or App controls to stop reminders or unlink the companion. If Telegram does not show a verified linked state, return to the full [Stack Labs App](https://app.stacklabs.bio/) and start the linking flow again.
