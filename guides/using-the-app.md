# Using the app

After signing in, the app brings the main Stack Labs activities into one place. You can create and review stacks, record a daily check-in, and follow progress through the current reward feedback.

Labs provides a private workflow for supported records and review steps. Insights presents informational summaries based on available product data. These features are designed to help with organization and reflection; they are not medical advice and do not replace professional care.

Profile and account areas contain the controls that are currently available for your session and product preferences. If the beta interface changes, follow the labels and guidance shown in the current app.
