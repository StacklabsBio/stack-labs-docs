# Platform overview

> Public integration contracts are not available yet. This page explains the product surfaces only; it does not define a public API, webhook, or SDK.

Stack Labs is organized as one product platform with several client experiences. The public website, PWA, and Telegram companion can evolve and deploy independently while using a common service boundary for trusted product operations.

The PWA is the primary product client. The website handles public product communication, and the Telegram experience focuses on selected companion actions. This separation allows each interface to follow the expectations of its environment without duplicating the product's core authority.

Technical interfaces will be documented here only after they are stable, intentionally external, and ready for third-party use.
