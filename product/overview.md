# Product overview

Stack Labs is a product platform for organizing personal protocols, recording daily progress, and reviewing information over time. The current experience is built around a website, a progressive web app, and a Telegram companion.

The website explains the product and provides a direct route into the app. The PWA is the main interactive experience. It supports account access, stack tracking, daily check-ins, reward feedback, Labs workflows, and informational insights.

The Telegram companion offers a lighter way to reach selected daily actions. It complements the PWA rather than replacing the full product experience.

Stack Labs is under active development. Features, wording, and workflows may change as the product is tested and improved.
