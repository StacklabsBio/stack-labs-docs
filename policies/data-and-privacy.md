# Data and privacy

Stack Labs treats account and health-adjacent information as private product data. Authenticated information is intended for the signed-in experience and should not be exposed through public pages or shared caches.

The current App uses account controls and product boundaries designed to keep private activity associated with the correct user. Use the current App settings when reviewing the account and privacy options available to you.

Stack Labs provides organizational and informational features. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Formal legal policies and any region-specific rights will be published through an approved policy process rather than inferred from product documentation.
